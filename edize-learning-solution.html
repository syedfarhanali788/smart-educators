Common questions about EDIZE LEARNING SOLUTION

Our solution is designed to complement your existing curriculum, not replace it. We work with your faculty to align our competitive exam preparation modules with your school syllabus, ensuring students learn both simultaneously without extra burden.

The basic requirements are: Computer lab with internet access or tablets for students, projector in classrooms, and a stable internet connection. We provide detailed specifications and can assist with setting up the necessary infrastructure.

We conduct comprehensive training programs for your faculty, including: Initial 2-day intensive workshop, weekly virtual training sessions for first month, dedicated support team available 24/7, and detailed user manuals/video tutorials.

We currently offer programs for grades 8-12, covering preparation for all major competitive exams (NEET, JEE, CUET, etc.). The difficulty level and content are appropriately tailored for each grade level.

We take data security very seriously. Our platform complies with all applicable data protection regulations, uses bank-grade encryption, and stores all data on secure servers. Schools retain full ownership of their student data.
